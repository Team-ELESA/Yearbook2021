Message From HOD

Dr. S. K. Jagtap

HOD (E&TC)

Dearest Students,

It gives me great pleasure to write a few words as prologue for our first ever in-house yearbook ‘Reminiscence’. It is exclusively meant to cherish and preserve our memories for the year 2020-21. This yearbook acts as a platform to display creative thoughts and literary talents of many students. Team ELESA deserves a special appreciation for their efforts in compiling this publication.

The all-round progress of our students is of paramount importance and our most primary goal. It is indeed a matter of great pride that our students consistently excel in academic and co-curricular activities. I feel proud of being the Head of Electronics & Telecommunications Engineering Department of such a magnificent institution dedicated for the betterment of students.

The main focus of SKNCOE is to empower the students with sound knowledge, wisdom, experience and methodological training both at academic level and in the highly competitive global market. I take this opportunity to greet and commend all the students, faculty and administrative staff for creating and maintaining an aura of optimism at out department. I extend my wishes to all the readers of this message and wish you the best for scaling bigger success and achieving newer heights in the coming days.
